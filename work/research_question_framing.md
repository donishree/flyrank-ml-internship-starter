# Research Question Framing

## Provisional Lane
Lane 2 – Refresh / Content Opportunity Scoring

## Research Question
Which webpages should be prioritized for content refresh to reduce traffic decline?

## Why this question?
Large websites may contain thousands of webpages, making it impossible for SEO teams to manually review every page. A machine learning model can help identify which webpages should be refreshed first, allowing the team to focus on the highest-priority pages and make better use of their time.

## Unit of Analysis
Each individual webpage on the client's website.

## Model Output
The model will generate a ranked list of webpages along with a priority score indicating which pages should be refreshed first.

## Business Action
The SEO team will review the highest-priority webpages, update their content, and monitor their performance to improve search visibility and reduce traffic decline.

## Cost of a Wrong Recommendation
If the model recommends the wrong webpages, the SEO team may waste time updating less important pages while the webpages that truly need attention continue losing traffic.

## Why Machine Learning?
Machine learning can analyze thousands of webpages much faster than humans. It identifies patterns in historical search performance data and helps prioritize webpages that are most likely to benefit from content refresh.
